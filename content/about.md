The Norwegian Space Agency was established to coordinate and advance Norway's activities in space. With decades of experience, we have become a key player in the European space landscape.

{{< image-placeholder size="large-square" class="about-section-placeholder" >}}

### Our Mission

To develop and promote the use of space technology and science for the benefit of Norwegian society and contribute to global space exploration efforts.

### Core Values

- **Innovation** - Pushing the boundaries of what's possible
- **Excellence** - Maintaining the highest standards in all our endeavors
- **Collaboration** - Working together with partners worldwide
- **Sustainability** - Ensuring long-term benefits for future generations
